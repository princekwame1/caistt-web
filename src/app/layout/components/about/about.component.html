ABOUT US

CAISTT is an e-commerce website where agribusiness takes place. On CAISTT, businesses trade in agricultural machinery, farm inputs, seedlings, agrochemicals, etc with the ultimate aim of providing a market connection for all.

On the CAISTT platform, agribusiness stakeholders ranging from manufacturers and suppliers to farmers and distributors come together to engage in a wide array of trading activities. The platform's primary objective is to establish a comprehensive market connection for all participants, fostering an environment where agricultural products, machinery, and inputs can be bought and sold with ease
